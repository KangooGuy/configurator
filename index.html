Explanation

Beacon XL specifics:
Adv interval unit: 0.625 ms — e.g. 3200 = 2000 ms, 800 = 500 ms.
BLE MAC: big-endian (AA:BB:CC:DD:EE:FF, high byte first).
Beacon name: 20 bytes ASCII, null-padded to fill the field.
Location interval 0 = disable periodic GNSS+WiFi scans (one-shot via Runner 0x82 still works).

Tracking & Intervals

Moving and steady intervals combine with movement duration to derive total position fixes per day.
—
Fixes/day = movement_h × 3600 / moving_interval + (24 − movement_h) × 3600 / steady_interval
Outdoor fixes → assisted GNSS  ·  Indoor fixes → WiFi scan (or autonomous GNSS)
